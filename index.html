How I work

My approach to Solutions Engineering

Every solution I've delivered follows the same pattern. It's what I do naturally.

01
Find the real problem
Not the symptom someone reported. The actual bottleneck. I sit with the people doing the work and understand what's broken before touching a keyboard.
02
Build the right-sized solution
Sometimes it's a TypeScript script. Sometimes it's a full Django app. I pick the tool that fits the constraint — not the one that looks impressive on a spec sheet.
03
Demo, sign off, deploy
A solution nobody uses is a waste of time. I demo to leadership, get sign-off, train the team, and make sure it's adopted. That's when the work is done.
